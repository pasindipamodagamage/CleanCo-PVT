About us

Welcome to PreRental

At Carbook, we are dedicated to providing you with the best car rental experience in Sri Lanka. Whether you're exploring the bustling streets of Colombo, the cultural heritage of Kandy, or the serene beaches of Mirissa, we offer a wide range of vehicles to suit your needs.

Our mission is to make your journey seamless and enjoyable. With years of experience in the industry, we pride ourselves on offering reliable, affordable, and customer-focused services. From luxury sedans for special occasions to robust SUVs for family trips, we have the perfect vehicle for every adventure.
